## Description

The Wolff Timer is a beautifully designed interval timer built for circuit training, HIIT, Tabata, and any workout that needs precise timing.

SIMPLE & POWERFUL
Set your work time, rest time, rounds, and sets. Tap start. That's it. No complicated menus, no subscriptions, no ads.

LIVE ACTIVITY
See your timer right on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. Always know what's coming next without unlocking your phone.

WORKS WITH YOUR MUSIC
Audio cues automatically lower your music volume during beeps, then restore it. Your Spotify, Apple Music, or podcasts keep playing.

BACKGROUND RELIABLE
The timer keeps running accurately even when your phone is locked or you're using other apps. Never miss a transition.

PERFECT FOR

• Boxing rounds

• Any interval-based training
