Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a workout?

On the setup screen, use the wheel pickers to set your work time, rest time, number of rounds, and sets. Tap "START WORKOUT" when ready.

Does the timer work when my phone is locked?

Yes! The timer continues running in the background. You'll see the countdown on your Lock Screen via Live Activity, and hear audio cues for each transition.

Will the timer interrupt my music?

No. The audio cues briefly lower your music volume (audio ducking), then restore it automatically. Your music keeps playing.

How do I pause or resume from the Lock Screen?

Tap the pause/play button on the Live Activity widget shown on your Lock Screen or in the Dynamic Island.

Is my data collected?

No. The Wolff Timer does not collect any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device.
